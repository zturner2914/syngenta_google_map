Come Grow with Us

Syngenta currently has agreements in place with a growing number of ethanol plants, enabling them to cut costs by sourcing enzymes locally and be more competitive. As new plants come on board, Syngenta expects ethanol produced with Enogen corn enzyme technology to be approximately 2.5 billion gallons during 2018 alone.

Be an Enzyme Supplier

Since its introduction, Enogen corn enzyme technology has provided corn growers the opportunity to be enzyme suppliers for a growing number of ethanol plants and earn a per-bushel premium. Syngenta expects premiums-to-date paid to Enogen corn growers will surpass $100 million during 2018.
